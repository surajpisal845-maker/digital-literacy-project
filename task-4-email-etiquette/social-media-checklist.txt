1. Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

 Do’s:
Maintain a professional profile (clear bio, appropriate profile picture)
Think before posting (once posted, it stays online forever)
Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
Share useful and positive content related to academics or skills
Keep your privacy settings updated


 Don’ts:
Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or controversial content
Do not spread misinformation or fake news
Avoid excessive use of social media during study/work hours
Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
