Stay Safe Online – Checklist
Use strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers, symbols) and avoid using the same password everywhere
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts
Do not click on unknown links or suspicious emails/messages
Always verify website URLs before entering login or payment details
Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone
Regularly update your apps and system to fix security vulnerabilities
Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store
Log out from accounts on shared or public devices


 Financial / UPI Safety (IMPORTANT):
Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID/name before sending money
Never approve “collect requests” unless you are sure (receiving money does NOT require approval)
